Subnetting and Basics of NID and BID

NID signifies the collective representation of your network. NID cannot be assigned to any computer. Similar in case with BID i.e. Broadcast ID.

NID → Used to scan and get information of all computers in LAN at once. BID → Used to send a single message to all device within your LAN at once.
